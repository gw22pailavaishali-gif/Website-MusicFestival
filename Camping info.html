Scone Camping and Caravanning Club Site Information

Location: Adjacent to Perth Racecourse in Scone, Perthshire (PH2 6BB).

Facilities: Modern amenities including hot showers, family shower rooms, laundry facilities, a washing-up area, and a motorhome service point.

Pitches: 120, offering a mix of grass and hardstanding, many with electric hook-ups.

Atmosphere: Quiet and tranquil, with wildlife such as deer and owls spotted.

Access: It is within walking/cycling distance of Perth city center and situated next to the Perth Racecourse.

Operating Dates: Typically open from early March to early January.

Restrictions: One car per pitch; limited visitor parking.
